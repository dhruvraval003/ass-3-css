My Favorite Candy - KitKat

About KitKat

KitKat is a brand of chocolate-covered wafer confectionery that was introduced by Rowntree's of York, England in 1935. It is now produced globally by Nestlé, which acquired Rowntree's in 1988. KitKat is known for its four-finger shape and its slogan "Have a Break, Have a KitKat."

Fun Facts about KitKat

  • The original KitKat had only three fingers, but in 1958, a four-finger version was introduced in the UK.
  • KitKat is produced in many different flavors, including milk chocolate, white chocolate, dark chocolate, and matcha green tea.
  • In Japan, KitKat has become a popular souvenir because the name sounds similar to "kitto katsu," which means "surely win" in Japanese.

KitKat is produced by Nestlé, a Swiss multinational food and beverage company. For more information, visit our website at www.nestle.com.
